<p><p>The pad printing machine market can be segmented into two main types: oil pan type and oil cup type. The oil pan type features a larger reservoir, allowing for continuous ink circulation and efficient printing on flat or curved surfaces. Conversely, the oil cup type uses a smaller ink reservoir, making it more suitable for shorter production runs and precise ink control. Both types cater to various industries, offering distinct advantages based on production volume and printing requirements.</p></p>
