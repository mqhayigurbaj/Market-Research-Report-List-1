<p><strong>The Circular Polarizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Polarizers</li><li>Beam-splitting Polarizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The circular polarizer market comprises two main types: absorptive and beam-splitting polarizers. Absorptive polarizers selectively absorb light waves based on polarization, effectively blocking unwanted reflections and glare, making them ideal for photography and displays. Beam-splitting polarizers, on the other hand, divide incoming light into two orthogonally polarized beams, often used in optical systems and imaging applications. Both types enhance visual clarity and contrast, catering to various industries, including consumer electronics, photography, and scientific research.</p></p>
