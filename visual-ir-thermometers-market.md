<p><p>The Visual IR Thermometer market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact thermometers. Contact Visual IR Thermometers require physical touch to measure temperature, providing high accuracy for applications where proximity is safe and practical. Conversely, Non-contact Visual IR Thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, making them ideal for scenarios where contact is not feasible or safe, such as in hazardous environments or for quick assessments in medical settings.</p></p>
