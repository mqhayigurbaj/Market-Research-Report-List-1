<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resonance Technology</li><li>Inductive Charging Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless electric vehicle charger market primarily includes two technologies: magnetic resonance and inductive charging. Magnetic resonance technology utilizes magnetic fields to transfer energy between coils, allowing for efficient charging at greater distances compared to traditional methods. Inductive charging, on the other hand, relies on electromagnetic fields created by closely aligned coils, enabling energy transfer only when the vehicle is parked over the charging pad. Both technologies aim to enhance convenience and promote the adoption of electric vehicles.</p></p>
