<p><ul><li>Closed Loop System</li><li>Open Loop System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geothermal power utilizes heat from the Earth to generate electricity, whereas geothermal heat pumps are designed for heating and cooling buildings. The market is divided into two main systems: closed loop and open loop. Closed loop systems circulate fluid through underground pipes, maintaining a consistent temperature, making them efficient for residential and commercial use. Open loop systems draw groundwater directly, utilizing its thermal properties for heating or cooling, but they require sustainable water source management to avoid depletion.</p></p>
