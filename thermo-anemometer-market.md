<p><strong>The Thermo Anemometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Current Anemometer</li><li>Constant Pressure Anemometer</li><li>Thermostatic Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermo anemometer market encompasses three primary types: constant current anemometers, constant pressure anemometers, and thermostatic anemometers. Constant current anemometers maintain a consistent current through a sensor, allowing for precise flow measurements. Constant pressure anemometers operate by adjusting the pressure difference across a sensor to gauge airflow. Thermostatic anemometers utilize temperature differences to determine flow rate, leveraging heat transfer principles. Each type serves different applications in HVAC, environmental monitoring, and industrial settings, enhancing airflow analysis and control.</p></p>
