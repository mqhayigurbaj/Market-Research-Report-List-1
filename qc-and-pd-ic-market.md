<p><p>The QC and PD IC market encompasses three primary types of ports: Downstream Facing Port (DFP), Upstream Facing Port (UFP), and Dual Role Port (DRP). DFPs supply power to devices, typically acting as outputs; UFPs receive power and data from devices, functioning as inputs. DRPs combine both functionalities, allowing for flexible power distribution and data transfer, adapting to various device requirements. This segmentation caters to diverse technology applications, enhancing compatibility and efficiency in power delivery and communication.</p></p>
