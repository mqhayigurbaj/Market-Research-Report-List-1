<p><p>The electrolysis of water market primarily comprises traditional alkaline electrolysers and proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolysers. Traditional alkaline electrolysers utilize a liquid alkaline solution as the electrolyte, offering cost-effective solutions for large-scale hydrogen production. In contrast, PEM electrolysers employ a solid polymer membrane, providing higher efficiency and faster response times, making them ideal for applications with variable renewable energy sources. Both technologies are crucial for advancing hydrogen production and supporting the shift towards sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
