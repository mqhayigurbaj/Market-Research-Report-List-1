<p><strong>The Cultured Whey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Isolate</li><li>Whey Hydrolysate</li><li>Whey Concentrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cultured whey market consists of three main types: whey isolate, whey hydrolysate, and whey concentrate. Whey isolate contains the highest protein content and minimal lactose, making it ideal for those seeking rapid absorption. Whey hydrolysate undergoes pre-digestion for faster assimilation and is often used in sports nutrition. Whey concentrate offers a balanced protein source with some fat and carbohydrates, appealing to general consumers and those looking for a cost-effective option. Each type serves different dietary needs and preferences.</p></p>
