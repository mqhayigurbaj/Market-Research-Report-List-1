<p><p>Shrink bundlers are categorized into three market types based on their operation and automation level. Automatic shrink bundlers are fully automated systems that efficiently wrap products without manual intervention, enhancing speed and productivity. Semi-automatic shrink bundlers require some manual setup but automate key processes, offering a balance between efficiency and control. Manual shrink bundlers rely entirely on human operation, providing flexibility for low-volume or customized packaging needs, though they may be slower and less consistent than automated options.</p></p>
